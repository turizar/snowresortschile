Discover Portillo Ski Resort

Located in the Andes Mountains, around 164 kilometers from Santiago, Portillo Ski Resort is one of the oldest and most prestigious ski resorts in South America. Known for its stunning views of the Inca Lagoon and the surrounding mountains, Portillo offers a unique and world-renowned experience for winter sports enthusiasts.

Portillo has been a destination for international skiers and snowboarders for decades, offering an incredible range of slopes, including those suitable for all levels. The resort has a rich history, hosting international ski events and drawing celebrities and athletes from around the world. It is known for its high-altitude ski slopes, some of the best snow conditions in the region, and its secluded, intimate atmosphere.

The resort offers not only skiing and snowboarding, but also a variety of services, including ski rentals, ski schools, and guided tours of the slopes. With a focus on creating a community atmosphere, Portillo is ideal for families, groups, and those looking for a more private, exclusive skiing experience in a breathtaking mountain setting.

Ski Slopes

>Portillo offers a variety of slopes suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced skiers, ensuring a fulfilling experience for everyone.

Chairlifts

The resort has 14 lifts, including a mix of chairlifts, T-bars, and a funicular, providing excellent access to the entire mountain.

Accommodation & Spa

Portillo offers world-class accommodations with stunning mountain views, a relaxing spa, and several dining options.

Discover Nevados de Chillán Ski Resort

Located in the Andes, approximately 80 km east of Chillán in southern Chile, Nevados de Chillán is one of the country's most famous ski resorts. The resort stands at an altitude of 3,200 meters at its highest point, offering a wide range of slopes and terrain for skiers and snowboarders of all levels. With over 30 kilometers of skiable terrain and modern infrastructure, it is a top destination for winter sports enthusiasts.

Nevados de Chillán is unique not only for its excellent snow conditions but also for its volcanic setting. The resort is located near the active Chillán Volcano, which adds a distinct character to the surrounding landscape. In addition to skiing, the area offers stunning views of the volcano and the surrounding wilderness, making it a perfect location for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

The resort features high-quality services such as ski rentals, ski schools, and guided mountain tours. Visitors can enjoy a variety of slopes ranging from beginner to expert level, as well as activities such as snowboarding, snowshoeing, and even heliskiing. In addition to skiing, Nevados de Chillán is known for its thermal baths, which provide a relaxing way to unwind after a day on the slopes.

Ski Slopes

Nevados de Chillán offers more than 30 kilometers of slopes for all levels, including some challenging terrains for advanced skiers.

Chairlifts

The resort has 14 lifts, including a modern gondola and several chairlifts, providing access to the mountain's various zones.

Accommodation & Spa

The resort offers a variety of accommodations, including luxury hotels and cabins, and features thermal hot springs for relaxation after skiing.

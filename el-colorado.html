Discover El Colorado Ski Resort

El Colorado Ski Resort, located just 36 kilometers from Santiago, is one of the most popular ski resorts in Chile. Nestled in the Andes Mountains, El Colorado offers over 1,000 hectares of skiable terrain, with slopes ranging from beginner to advanced levels, making it suitable for all types of skiers and snowboarders.

Founded in 1987, El Colorado is known for its excellent snow conditions and long ski season, which typically runs from June to October. With its close proximity to Santiago, the resort is easily accessible and attracts both locals and international visitors. It is a great destination for those looking to enjoy both skiing and the beautiful mountain landscapes.

El Colorado Ski Resort offers a variety of services, including ski rentals, ski schools, and guided tours. Apart from skiing and snowboarding, visitors can enjoy other activities like snowshoeing and snowmobiles. The resort also features a variety of accommodations and dining options, making it a perfect spot for both families and adventure seekers.

Ski Slopes

El Colorado offers over 30 ski slopes, with terrain for all levels, including wide beginner slopes and challenging runs for advanced skiers.

Chairlifts

The resort is equipped with modern lifts, including high-speed chairlifts and a gondola, ensuring fast access to all slopes.

Accommodation & Spa

El Colorado offers a range of accommodations, from cozy lodges to luxury hotels, and features a relaxing spa for post-ski treatments.

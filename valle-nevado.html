Discover Valle Nevado Ski Resort

Located in the heart of the Andes, Valle Nevado Ski Resort is one of the largest and most popular ski resorts in South America. The resort spans over 900 hectares of skiable terrain, offering a variety of slopes suitable for skiers and snowboarders of all levels. Valle Nevado is known for its high-altitude slopes and stunning views of the Andes mountains.

Founded in 1988, Valle Nevado has established itself as a top destination for winter sports in the Southern Hemisphere. The resort is located at the foot of Mount El Plomo, one of the most iconic mountains in Chile. Its prime location ensures excellent snow conditions throughout the ski season, attracting visitors from around the world.

Valle Nevado Ski Resort offers a wide range of services, including ski rentals, ski schools, and guided tours. In addition to skiing and snowboarding, the resort features activities such as snowshoeing and snowmobiles. There is also a relaxing spa, ideal for unwinding after a long day on the slopes, making it a great destination for both families and adventure seekers.

Ski Slopes

Valle Nevado offers over 30 ski slopes, with terrain for all skill levels. The resort features more than 60 kilometers of runs, ensuring an unforgettable skiing experience for everyone.

Chairlifts

The resort features 14 modern chairlifts, including high-speed quad lifts and a gondola, providing easy access to all areas of the mountain. With efficient lift systems, guests can quickly reach the top of the slopes, minimizing wait times and maximizing time on the snow.

Accommodation & Spa

Valle Nevado offers a variety of accommodation options, from luxurious hotels to cozy lodges, all with spectacular mountain views. The resort also features a relaxing spa to unwind after skiing.

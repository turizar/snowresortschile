Discover Corralco Ski Resort

Located in the Malalcahuello-Nalcas National Reserve, at the foot of the Lonquimay Volcano, Corralco Ski Resort offers a unique experience in the Andean region of Chile. The resort spans over 1,800 hectares of skiable terrain, ranging in altitude from 1,440 meters to 2,400 meters above sea level. Whether you're a beginner, intermediate, or expert skier, Corralco offers something for everyone, with its wide range of slopes and modern amenities.

Founded in 2007, Corralco has quickly become one of the premier ski destinations in the country. The resort's location within the scenic Malalcahuello National Reserve provides breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape, including the majestic Lonquimay Volcano, which is one of the tallest volcanoes in the Chilean Andes. The natural beauty of the area, combined with its excellent facilities, makes Corralco a must-visit destination for winter sports enthusiasts.

Corralco Ski Resort offers a variety of services, including ski rentals, ski schools, and guided tours, allowing visitors to fully enjoy their time on the mountain. In addition to skiing and snowboarding, the resort also offers a range of other activities, such as snowshoeing, snowmobiles, and a relaxation area with a spa, making it the perfect destination for families and adventure seekers alike.

Ski Slopes

Corralco offers 29 ski slopes for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced skiers, with stunning views of the Lonquimay Volcano.

Chairlifts

The resort features six chairlifts, including two double chairlifts, ensuring easy access to all ski slopes.

Accommodation & Spa

Corralco provides cozy accommodations with mountain views and a relaxing spa for post-ski treatments like massages and thermal baths.

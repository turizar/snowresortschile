Discover La Parva Ski Resort

La Parva Ski Resort is located just 40 kilometers from Santiago, Chile, and sits at an elevation of 2,750 meters above sea level. The resort is part of the "Tres Valles" (Three Valleys) ski area, which also includes Valle Nevado and El Colorado, providing access to more than 100 kilometers of slopes. With over 30 ski runs, La Parva caters to skiers and snowboarders of all skill levels, from beginners to experts.

La Parva is well known for its challenging terrain, which is particularly popular among intermediate and advanced skiers. The resort offers excellent snow conditions and stunning views of the Andes Mountains. Its location in the Farellones Valley provides a unique setting for winter sports enthusiasts looking for both adventure and breathtaking natural scenery.

The resort features a variety of services to make your stay enjoyable, including ski rentals, ski schools, and a range of dining options. Whether you're here for a day trip or an extended stay, La Parva provides a variety of accommodation options, including luxury apartments and cozy lodgings, all offering spectacular mountain views. Ski Slopes

La Parva offers 30 slopes across 38 km, catering to all skill levels, with panoramic views of the Andes.

Chairlifts

The resort features 14 ski lifts, including 2 quadruple-chair lifts, ensuring efficient access to all slopes.

Accommodation & Spa

La Parva provides a range of accommodations, from luxury apartments to more affordable options, along with spa facilities for relaxation after a day on the slopes.
